Aluminum Waste Stats

A quick look at why aluminum recovery matters—especially at venues like stadiums and concerts.

~50% of Cans Recycled

Only about half of aluminum beverage cans in the U.S. are recycled, with the rest ending up in landfills or as waste.

EPA: Containers & Packaging Data

80,000 lbs of Waste per Game

Large stadium events can produce over 80,000 pounds of waste, much of it from beverage containers.

EPA: Sustainable Materials Management

95% Energy Savings

Recycling aluminum uses about 95% less energy than producing new aluminum from raw materials.

Aluminum Association: Recycling
